Why Me?

"A great effort, worth it with the best." - @Me

In simple words, it is a goal to be at the most relevant level in the IT industry, and what better than you. When I was a college student, it was a young dream.

But now I think it's an attainable achievement because I'm pretty sure I have some skills to share with co-workers, knowledge to bring to the team, and a lot to grow with the company. I would like to get the opportunity to show what I got. I'm an integrated person full of respect and compromise values, I really like the challenges and I can give good solutions.

In the meanwhile, I'll keep improving my skills and getting more knowledge in order to be ready for the next cool challenge.
